Self-Assessment

After a discussion, write 2–3 sentences answering:

  • Which roles did you take up? Provide a specific example.
  • Where did you move learning forward? Cite a moment.
  • What will you try next time to improve equity and clarity?

Evidence of Impact

  • Quote a peer and explain how you built on it.
  • Point to a textual moment you clarified for the group.
  • Name one time you invited a quieter voice in.
