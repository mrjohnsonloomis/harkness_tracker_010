Pre-Discussion Checklist

  • Annotate the text: questions, confusions, striking lines.
  • Draft 2 interpretive questions (invite multiple answers).
  • Tag 3–5 quotes with page numbers for likely themes.
  • Choose a starting role (e.g., Initiator or Evidence Finder).

Question Crafting Prompts

  • “How does the author’s choice to ___ shape our view of ___?”
  • “What tension emerges between ___ and ___, and why?”
  • “If we rethink ___ through the lens of ___, what changes?”
